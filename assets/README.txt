Put your images here.

  profile.jpg   -> your photo (square works best, e.g. 600x600). Shown in the hero.
                   If missing, the site shows a gradient monogram of your initials instead.
  resume.pdf    -> your resume (optional). Linked from the hero "Résumé" button.
                   To hide that button, set `resume: null` in ../src/data.js.

You can use .png instead of .jpg — just update the `photo` path in ../src/data.js.
